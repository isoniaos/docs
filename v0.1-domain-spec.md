## 2. Hard rules

### Tenant isolation
Ни одна сущность не должна пересекать границы `orgId`.

### Source of truth
Полномочия, route и статус proposal проверяются контрактами, а не backend.

### Off-chain role
Backend только агрегирует и объясняет.

## 3. Demo topologies

## A. Simple DAO+
Bodies:
- General Council
- Treasury Committee
- Security Council

Rules:
- `STANDARD`: General Council
- `TREASURY`: Treasury Committee + General Council, veto Security Council, timelock 1h
- `UPGRADE`: General Council + Security Council, timelock 2h
- `EMERGENCY`: Security Council immediate path

## B. Bicameral preview
Bodies:
- Capital House
- Merit House
- Emergency Council

Rules:
- `TREASURY`: Capital House
- `UPGRADE`: Merit House + Emergency veto window
- `EMERGENCY`: Emergency Council
