## 2. Hard rules

### Tenant isolation

No entity may cross `orgId` boundaries.

### Source of truth

Proposal authority, route, and status are enforced by contracts, not by the backend.

### Off-chain role

The backend only aggregates and explains.

## 3. Demo topologies

## A. Simple DAO+

Bodies:

- General Council
- Treasury Committee
- Security Council

Rules:

- `STANDARD`: General Council
- `TREASURY`: Treasury Committee + General Council, veto Security Council, timelock 1h
- `UPGRADE`: General Council + Security Council, timelock 2h
- `EMERGENCY`: Security Council immediate path

## B. Bicameral preview

Bodies:

- Capital House
- Merit House
- Emergency Council

Rules:

- `TREASURY`: Capital House
- `UPGRADE`: Merit House + Emergency veto window
- `EMERGENCY`: Emergency Council
