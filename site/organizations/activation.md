# Activation

Activation is the process that moves an organization from setup/bootstrap into its intended governance authority model.

## Why activation matters

During setup, a project may need temporary administrative powers to configure roles, policy routes, voting modules, and initial governance bodies.

After setup, authority should move to the organization's intended governance process where supported.

Activation should make clear:

- who can configure the organization before activation;
- what permissions are granted;
- what governance bodies become authoritative;
- whether admin handoff is complete;
- which lifecycle state the organization is in;
- whether any emergency or break-glass powers remain.

## Public display

App Core and public archive pages should show activation/finalization status where the protocol supports it.

If the status is unsupported, stale, or unavailable, the UI should say so rather than hiding uncertainty.

## Current status

Activation and admin-handoff details are being normalized in the protocol and runtime docs. This page explains the model at a high level until repository-specific commands are current.
