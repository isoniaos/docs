# Accountability

Accountability is the product center of IsoniaOS.

A passed proposal creates obligations, not just a result.

## Accountability records

An accountability record can track:

- responsible party;
- due date;
- execution status;
- linked transaction hash;
- external proof;
- milestone status;
- blocker or failure reason;
- cancellation reason;
- completion review;
- source and trust-boundary labels.

## Status model

Useful accountability states include:

- not required;
- awaiting execution;
- in progress;
- blocked;
- executed;
- completed;
- failed;
- cancelled.

## Evidence levels

Evidence can mature gradually:

- manual status update;
- linked proof;
- imported proof metadata;
- verified proof;
- native or directly executed action.

Manual records remain valuable, but the UI must distinguish them from verified evidence and contract state.
